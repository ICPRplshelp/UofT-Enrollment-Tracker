All undergraduate courses from the Faculty of Arts and Science, APS and Engineering, Daniels, UTM, and UTSC are supported.

This site does not update in realtime and cannot tell you how quickly waitlists drop.

  • All dates and deadlines are based on the Faculty of Arts and Science, even if the course visible in the chart isn't offered by FAS. Double check if your Faculty's deadlines and policies are the same as FAS!! UTSC tends to be inconsistent.
  • Course information is sourced from here.
  • If a lecture section is added late, it will have a cap of 0 before it was added. If a section is cancelled, the enrollment cap will be set to 0 the time it was cancelled.
  • Refund information is only shown for summer courses as course fees always apply there for FAS students. During the Fall-Winter term, most students pay program fees (which means students taking 4 or more credits throughout the year pay for the price of 5, and no refunds if they remain in that range)
  • Quickly navigate through sessions/terms by pressing COMMA (,) or PERIOD (.) on your keyboard while focused on the course input text box.
  • Your enrollment time of day is determined by your admission category (year 1) or credits you have. For year 2-3 students, you'll get an earlier time if you have more credits. For year 4 students, you'll get an earlier time if you have less credits. The earliest possible enrollment time is 9AM.
